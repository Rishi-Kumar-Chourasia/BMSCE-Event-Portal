About BMSCE Events

BMSCE Events is an integrated event management platform developed to enhance communication and coordination between students and club moderators of BMS College of Engineering. The portal enables students to seamlessly discover and register for various club activities, technical workshops, and cultural events, while providing authorized club representatives with an efficient interface to post, manage, and monitor their events. By centralizing event information and registrations, BMSCE Events aims to foster active student participation, streamline organizational processes, and strengthen the collaborative spirit within the BMSCE community.
